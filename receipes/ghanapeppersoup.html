Ghanapepper Soup

Ghana mixes with Nigeria in soup

Ghanapepper is one which was born from fusion of two beautiful countries.

ingredients

  • ehuru
  • uda
  • pepper,tomatoes and onions
  • fresh fish
  • boulion cubes
  • crayfish

steps

  1. blend pepper and tomatoes then sieve
  2. boil sieved juice
  3. add in meat stock if any, seasoning, ehuru and uda seeds
  4. add in already washed fish
  5. boil till a little thickned
  6. taste for necessary ingredients
  7. make starch or purchase already made one
  8. bring down soup, serve and enjoy
