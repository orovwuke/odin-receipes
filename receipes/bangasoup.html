Banga Soup

Delicious Bangasoup and stach

The bangasoup is a traditional meal for the Urhobo people of delta state, Nigeria.

ingredients

  • palmnut fruits
  • bangasoup spices
  • meat or fish
  • meat stock
  • boulion cubes
  • starch

steps

  1. boil banga fruits for about 30mins
  2. sieve fruits and pound in a mortar
  3. squeeze out the juices from the fruits
  4. boil juice also adding in the meat stock,bollion cubes and spices
  5. taste for ingredients
  6. allow juice to boil down and become thickened
  7. make starch or purchase already made one
  8. bring down soup, serve and enjoy
