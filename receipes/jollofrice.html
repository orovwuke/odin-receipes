Jollof Rice

The best jollof in the house

the delicious giant of africa.

ingredients

  • pepper,onions and tomatoes
  • meat
  • rice
  • groundnut-oil
  • boulion cubes
  • bayleaf
  • white and black pepper
  • curry and thyme
  • tomato paste

steps

  1. season meat boil and fry it
  2. using groundnut oil from meat, fry sliced onions until fragrant
  3. add in curry, thyme, bayleaf, peppers
  4. put in pasted tomatoes
  5. pour in already blended pepper and tomatoes
  6. put salt, boulions and other seasonings
  7. allow to fry till all water has evarporated
  8. taste for necessary ingredients
  9. pour in rice which has been properly washed
  10. add in water and cover till cooked
  11. serve alongside fried meat enjoyyy
